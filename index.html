How to Play

Setup

Each player has 6 pieces: 2 small, 2 medium, 2 large. You play Blue 🔵, AI plays Red 🔴.

Your Turn

  • Place a piece from your reserve: tap a reserve piece, then tap a valid board cell.
  • Move a piece on the board: tap your visible piece, then tap a valid destination.

Gobbling

  • A larger piece can cover a smaller piece of either color.
  • Only the top (visible) piece in each cell counts toward winning.
  • Covered pieces may reappear if the top piece moves away!

Winning

Get 3 of your visible pieces in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Tip

Watch out — the AI can gobble your pieces and steal your winning line!
